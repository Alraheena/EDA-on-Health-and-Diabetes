# EDA-on-Health-and-Diabetes
This is an Exploratory Data Analysis project on how health, lifestyle and clinical factors leads to a person diabetic or not ,here analysed the problem ,cause and its patterns and trends .This dataset captures detailed health, lifestyle, and clinical information from a large population of individuals, designed to analyze and predict diabetes risk. Each record represents a comprehensive profile of a patient, including demographic attributes, lifestyle behaviors, family medical background, physiological measurements, and clinical test results.

The dataset includes variables such as age, gender, ethnicity, education level, income, employment status, smoking and alcohol habits, diet and physical activity, sleep patterns, body mass index (BMI), blood pressure, cholesterol levels, glucose, insulin, HbA1c, and family or personal medical history. Most importantly, it contains target variables — diagnosed_diabetes (binary: 0 = No, 1 = Yes) and diabetes_stage (categorical: No Diabetes, Pre-Diabetes, Type 1, Type 2, Gestational) — indicating the presence and stage of diabetes.

The objective of analyzing this dataset is to uncover patterns and risk factors associated with diabetes. By examining correlations between demographic characteristics, lifestyle choices, and clinical measurements, this analysis aims to provide insights into high-risk populations, lifestyle interventions, and potential predictive indicators to support better health management and disease prevention strategies.
